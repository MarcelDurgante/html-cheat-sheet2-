Strong

The strong element is appropriate to mark with special emphasis on the most important parts of a text.
its labels are: <strong> </strong>

Strikethrough

The HTML element places a strikethrough (horizontal line) over the text.
its labels are: <strike> </strike>



Exemple:

the money it is importan but health its more.

Italics

Subscript

The HTML element defines a piece of text that must be displayed, for typographic reasons, lower, and generally smaller, than the main section of the text, that is, in subscript
its labels are: <sub> </sub>



Exemple:

the money it is importan but health its more.
